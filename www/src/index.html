Kinshasa Collection

A web series and fashion brand between Kinshasa, Guangzhou and Berlin hijacking cultural clichés, stealing its audience for a roller coaster ride through real and fake, piracy and appropriation. The hot, steamy and unforgettably entertaining affair between the famous fashionista-guerrillas of Congo and China outpaces a German film team - that came to the new fashion capital Kinshasa to shoot a trailer for a campaign aiming to change the German's public perspective on Africa but eventually ends up in bed with a head turning new fashion brand.

Kinshasa Collection is produced by PONG FILM GmbH and co-produced by GOETHE-INSTITUT KINSHASA. Funded by the TURN FUND OF THE GERMAN FEDERAL CULTURAL FOUNDATION (Kulturstiftung des Bundes) as well as by MEDIENBOARD BERLIN-BRANDENBURG and HAUS DER KULTUREN DER WELT.

The Plotline

Episode #1 - Berlin

An influential German agency – DIE AGENTUR – with close ties to the government and some big foundations prepares the new major campaign “Africa on Eye Level” to promote Africa as the continent of unknown (economic) opportunities. One of the finalists for the campaign is a film team with a project called “Kinshasa Collection” showcasing Kinshasa as the up-and-coming fashion capital of Africa. However, if the film team wants to win this competition, they need to deliver a glossy trailer within two months.

Episode airs July 28, 2017

Episode #2 - Kinshasa

With very limited financial resources, the film team heads out on a research trip to Kinshasa only to discover that they are by far not the only foreign film team wanting to portray the city’s fashionistas. Even inflight-magazines have already created an exotic stereotype – which is what the film team wants to avoid. When the film team is forced to dump their plans, they frantically start searching for new storylines – until a meeting with Kinshasa based designers inspires them to book a flight to China.

Episode airs August 4, 2017

Episode #3 - Guangzhou

Heading to Guangzhou, China the team is confronted with the fact that the vivid African life of Guangzhou has become a magnet for filmmakers around the world. And yet another surprise is waiting for them: The Congolese partners don’t want the film team to accompany their shopping tour – too complicated plus the presence of “Whities” will increase the prices. How will they now be able to accomplish their extensive shopping list and get back to Kinshasa without being caught by customs?

Episode airs August 11, 2017

Episode #4 - Kinshasa

Back in Kinshasa and only 10 days left to deliver the trailer for the “Africa on Eye Level” campaign. Amidst frantic prep for shooting the film team gets a surprising phone call: Representatives of the German textile industry that partially finances the “Africa on Eye Level” campaign decided to disqualify “Kinshasa Collection” for promoting brand piracy. The team panics, facing their own bankruptcy. Turning the film project into a fashion enterprise by creating a new label – “Kinshasa Collection” – seems to be the only way out…

Episode airs August 18, 2017

Episode #5 - Berlin

Clothes and accessories have been shipped to Berlin from Kinshasa where models, and fashion influencers are ready to showcase the brand new label “Kinshasa Collection” for the big fashion show at one of Berlin’s coolest venues: The rooftop at Haus der Kulturen der Welt.

Episode airs August 25, 2017
